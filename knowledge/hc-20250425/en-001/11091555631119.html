How can I merge my multiple registered accounts?

MacroMicro offers two methods to sign up/login: "Social Account" and "Email Account." Each method creates a separate account, recognized as separate accounts by the system.

1. Merging a "Social Account" and an "Email Account"

Log in using your "Social Account." The system will detect if your "Email Account" uses the same email address and proceed with the merge.

2. Merging two "Social Accounts"

Log in using one of the "Social Accounts." The system will detect if another "Social Account" with the same email exists and proceed with the merge.

 

If the merge doesn’t happen automatically, feel free to contact us at support@macromicro.me.
