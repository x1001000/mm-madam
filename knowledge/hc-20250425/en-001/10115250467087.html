Why is the data not downloadable?

There are a few possible reasons why data cannot be downloaded:

  • You have not agreed to the MacroMicro Data Usage Agreement.
  • The team members have exceeded the shared monthly data download quota (500 entries per month for the yearly plan, 200 entries per month for the monthly plan).
  • Certain data provided through partnerships with MacroMicro's financial data partners cannot be downloaded due to contractual restrictions.
