What does the gray shading in the charts represent?

The gray shading marks periods of recessions as identified by the National Bureau of Economic Research (NBER), which is a key source of information for professional institutions around the world. For more information, please see here.

You can "show/hide" the background shading by clicking the ”Custom“ button at the top right of the chart. MM Prime subscribers can further unlock the full toolbox features, allowing the addition of shading for selected time periods to personalize charts.
