Can I get a refund if I am charged after my free trial ends?

If you decide not to continue with a subscription after your trial, please make sure to cancel it under "Payment History" before the trial ends.

If you forget to cancel before the trial expires, please contact our support team at support@macromicro.me before your trial period ends. Our team will review your case and provide assistance, including whether a refund may be granted under certain circumstances.
