How can I sign up for an account?

MacroMicro offers two methods to sign up/login:

 

Social login

You can quickly sign up/login through your social account. Currently, the following social platforms support this service:

 

Email Address

You can create a regular membership account using any common email address and setting your password.
After creating an account, the system will send an account registration verification email. Upon receiving the email, please ensure to click the verification button to ​activate a new accounts.

 

Reminder

Accounts created using different login methods will be recognized as separate accounts by the system.If you want to add other login methods to the same account after registration, please log in using the original login method, and then go to "Member Profile" settings to add other login methods.

 

Duplicate account problem or require further assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us by submitting the form.
