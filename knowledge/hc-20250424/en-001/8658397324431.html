I'm using the free trial. Why did I receive a payment confirmation email?

MacroMicro’s free trial includes automatic renewal, ensuring uninterrupted access to our services.

After the trial period ends, your subscription will automatically renew on a monthly or yearly basis (depending on the promotion), with the first payment processed on the same day your trial ends.

If you decide not to continue with the subscription, you can cancel anytime via Member Profile > Payment History.
Please make sure to cancel at least one day before your trial ends to avoid being charged.
