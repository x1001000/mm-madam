I've subscribed to the monthly plan. How can I switch to the annual subscription plan?

To switch to the annual plan for those paying with an agreed credit card, click on the profile picture in the top right corner, go to Member Profile, and click on Payment History, where you can “Upgrade to yearly plan". The annual subscription plan will be activated at the upcoming billing date.

 

 

If you're currently paying by a method other than a credit card, please reach out to MacroMicro Support Team at support@macromicro.me for assistance.
