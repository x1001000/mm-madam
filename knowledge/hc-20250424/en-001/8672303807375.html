What do team plans entail?

MacroMicro offers two team plans: "MM Prime" and "MM Business."

 

MM Prime Team Plan

  • Subscribe to six or more annual MM Prime plans at once to enjoy a 10% discount on the annual subscription price.
  • After subscribing to the MM Prime Team Plan, the purchaser (as the main account of the team plan) can manage/add other users (as sub-accounts of the team plan) to the team in "Member Profile," and they will all enjoy MM Prime privileges.

MM Business Team Plan

  • The subscription offers full historical data downloads, watermark removal, and complete authorization for commercial use of charts.
  • After subscribing to the MM Prime Team Plan, the purchaser (as the main account) can manage/add other users (as sub-accounts) to the team in "Member Profile," and all members will enjoy MM Prime privileges.
