What are the MM Fundamental Indices? How is it constructed?

About MM Fundamental Indices (MMFIs)

MacroMicro has compiled several key indices, known as the MM Fundamental Indices (MMFIs), leveraging the power of quant analysis. These indices are showcased in the 'Charts > Trader's Insight' section of the website, covering global stocks, currencies, bonds, and commodities. They provide users with a quick way to grasp the fundamentals of different asset classes.

How to grasp MMFIs

Users can look at the value and overall trend of each MM Fundamental Index. A high index value indicates strong fundamentals, and an upward trend indicates improving fundamentals.

How is it constructed

The MMFIs reflect the fundamental conditions of commodities, and relevant charts of their components are consolidated in the respective commodity's chart collection. For example, data used in compiling the MM US Dollar Fundamental Index is included in its chart collection. Weightings are adjusted automatically according to market conditions for real-time tracking.

When will the MMFIs be updated or revised in the future?

The Fundamental Index is updated on the last Friday of each month, reflecting the fundamentals over the past month. There may be subsequent changes due to data revisions for its constituent variables.
